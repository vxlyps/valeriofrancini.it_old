About Me

My name is Valerio Francini, I am 26 years old, born and raised in Rome.

I am an Independent Director and Screenwriter. I started young with analog photography, quickly growing passionate about cinema and storytelling. In recent years, I have written and directed numerous music videos and short films (this is one of the latest projects I'm proud of, shot entirely on 16mm and Super8 film: Maskara - Ycon Ydol 🎞️).

I studied Philosophy, a path seemingly distant from cinema, but one I chose to form myself as a person and nurture my vision of the world and reality ✨.
